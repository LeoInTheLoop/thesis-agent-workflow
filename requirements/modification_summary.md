# Modification Summary

> Historical record of all significant changes to workflow files, guidelines, and paper structure.
> When merging or deprecating an old file, summarize its content here before deletion.

---

## Format

Each entry should include:
- **Date**: YYYY-MM-DD
- **File(s) affected**: list of filenames
- **Type of change**: restructure / terminology update / citation rule / guideline addition / file merge
- **Summary**: what changed and why
